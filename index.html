About

Athena was born in Zakynthos, a beautiful island in Greece, and from the first years of her life she developed a special relationship with painting. She was "illustrating" books and her highschool notebooks with shapes and forms and dreamed of studying in School of Architecture or School of Fine Arts. However, dreams didn't come true, under the preasure of her parents she attended the School of Law and after graduation, she worked as Administrator at the University of the Aegean. All these years her passion for painting simmered. She dealt with jewelry, experimented with all kinds of materials, until a few years ago when she felt that the "punishment" was over, and started the exciting journey of lines and colors.
